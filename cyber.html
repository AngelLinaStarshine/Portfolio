"Symmetric Encryption & Practical Attack Mitigation: AES, 3DES, and Modern Attacks"

In this project, symmetric encryption is implemented using AES and 3DES, demonstrating how these algorithms encrypt and decrypt data. The project highlights the vulnerabilities of 3DES, particularly its susceptibility to brute-force attacks, and compares it to the more secure AES. It also covers countermeasures to mitigate attacks on symmetric encryption, focusing on key management practices, and demonstrates encryption using different block sizes and modes CBC and GCM.
